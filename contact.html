Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about dining with us

We welcome both walk-ins and reservations. However, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially on weekends and holidays, as we tend to get quite busy. You can book online through our website or by calling us directly.

Yes, we're happy to accommodate dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergies. Please inform your server about any dietary needs when you arrive, or note them in your reservation. Our chefs will prepare dishes that meet your requirements without compromising on flavor.

For regular reservations, we request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. For large groups (6+ people) and private events, we require 48 hours notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee. During peak seasons or special events, different policies may apply which will be communicated at the time of booking.

We offer valet parking service from 5PM to midnight daily. There is also street parking available nearby, and a public parking garage two blocks away. For private events, we can arrange dedicated parking for your guests - please inquire when booking your event.
